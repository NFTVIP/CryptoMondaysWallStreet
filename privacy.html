Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

CryptoMondays Wall Street ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website cryptomondayswallstreet.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or attend our events. Please read this policy carefully. If you disagree with its terms, please discontinue use of our site.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

  • Name, email address, and professional information submitted through event registration on Luma
  • Information submitted through contact or partnership inquiry forms
  • Speaker applications and nominations

Information Collected Automatically

  • Log data including IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent
  • Cookie data for basic site functionality and analytics
  • Referring URLs and device information

How We Use Your Information

We use information we collect to:

  • Process event registrations and communicate event details
  • Respond to inquiries and partnership requests
  • Send updates about upcoming events and programming (with your consent)
  • Improve our website and event offerings
  • Comply with legal obligations

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with:

  • Event platform providers (Luma) necessary to operate registrations
  • Service providers who assist in operating our website under confidentiality agreements
  • Law enforcement or regulatory bodies when required by law

Third-Party Services

Our website uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Luma — event registration and calendar management
  • CoinGecko — public cryptocurrency price data (no personal data shared)
  • Frankfurter / ECB — foreign exchange rate data (no personal data shared)
  • Google Fonts — typography delivery

Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law. Event registration data is managed through Luma and subject to their retention policies.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at j@nft-vip.io.

Cookies

We use essential cookies required for site functionality. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising. You may configure your browser to refuse cookies, though some site features may not function correctly as a result.

Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

Our site and events are intended for professionals and are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at j@nft-vip.io.
